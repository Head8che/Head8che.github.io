Finding Neemo

Wed 06 October 2021
By Danger Doggo
Category: Blog

"I Promise, I Will Never Let Anything Happen To You...Nemo." 🐬

“Just keep swimming.” This classic line from one of our favorite (albeit forgetful) fish friends has gotten us all through bad days aplenty. Ellen DeGeneres delivers a one-of-a-kind performance as Dory in Finding Nemo and the aptly-named sequel, Finding Dory. A dynamic duo, these two movies top our list for Friday night movies with the kids. But, c’mon, sometimes you just can’t wait till the weekend. If you need a little mid-week mood boost but don’t have time for a double-feature, we’ve got three words for you: Finding Nemo quotes. Because it’s always a good time to revisit some of the best lines from the cutest lucky-finned clownfish ever (AKA Nemo), along with Marlin, Dory, Crush, and the whole underwater gang.
