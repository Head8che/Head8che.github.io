Lion King

Tue 07 September 2021
By Danger Doggo
Category: Blog

Hakuna Matata. 🦁

As one of 2019’s most talked-about movies, Disney’s The Lion King was revived through a CGI remake last summer. Whether you’re a fan of the all-star remake or not, the 1994 classic continues to have a hold over our hearts (which is why we’re stoked we can watch both versions on Disney+). There are lessons to be learned – by both kids and adults! – about love, leadership, and friendship throughout the heartwarming film.
